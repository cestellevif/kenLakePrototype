Architectural Review Request

All exterior modifications to your home or property must be submitted to the Architectural Compliance Committee (ACC) for review and approval before work begins. This includes additions, fences, decks, sheds, changes to siding or paint color, and other visible modifications.

What happens next?

Your submission is reviewed by the appropriate board member or committee. Expect a response within 3–5 business days. For urgent matters, contact us directly at board@kenlake.org.
