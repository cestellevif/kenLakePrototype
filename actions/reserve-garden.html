Reserve a Community Garden Plot

Community garden plots at the Tot Lot are assigned annually by lottery. Interested residents may apply during the open application period (typically January through early April). Plot assignments are announced in April and are valid for one growing season.

What happens next?

Your submission is reviewed by the appropriate board member or committee. Expect a response within 3–5 business days. For urgent matters, contact us directly at board@kenlake.org.
