Reserve a Park or Recreation Area

Residents may reserve community spaces for private gatherings. Reservations are subject to availability and board approval. Please submit requests at least 2 weeks in advance. You will receive a confirmation email with instructions and any applicable guidelines.

What happens next?

Your submission is reviewed by the appropriate board member or committee. Expect a response within 3–5 business days. For urgent matters, contact us directly at board@kenlake.org.
