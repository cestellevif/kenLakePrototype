About This Committee

Events Committee

The Events Committee plans and coordinates neighborhood gatherings throughout the year, including the Annual Fishing Derby, the 4th of July Parade and Picnic, holiday events, and more. We love helping neighbors connect over shared celebrations.

Committees are formed by volunteer neighbors and approved by the board. All residents are welcome to participate. Contact us using the form to learn more or get involved.
